SCP-087 - The infinite moving step

SCP-087 is an unlit staircase located in a deserted building. Descend into the darkness and encounter the paranormal occurrences within. This endless stairwell is known for its terrifying manifestations and has left many explorers traumatized.

As you step down each creaking stair, the oppressive atmosphere grows thicker. Shadows dance along the walls, whispering voices echo in the void, and inexplicable coldness envelops your very being. Every step forward feels like a descent into madness.

Those who have braved the depths of SCP-087 report encountering spectral figures, grotesque apparitions, and unexplainable phenomena. Some claim to have heard anguished screams reverberating through the stairwell, while others speak of feeling an otherworldly presence watching their every move.

Researchers and paranormal investigators have attempted to uncover the secrets of SCP-087, but its depths remain shrouded in mystery. Each exploration only raises more questions and leaves its participants forever changed, their minds haunted by the horrors they have witnessed.
