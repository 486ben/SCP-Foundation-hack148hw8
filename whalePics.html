SCP Lab level 2

Once upon a time, in the secret and enigmatic halls of the SCP Foundation, there was a remarkable place known as the Archive Room on the second floor. This room held the collective knowledge of countless anomalous objects, creatures, and phenomena that had been meticulously documented and stored away for safekeeping. As the heavy double doors swung open, you entered the Archive Room, finding themselves surrounded by towering bookshelves, each laden with countless volumes. The air was thick with anticipation and the smell of aged paper. The room seemed to possess an aura of its own, whispering untold tales to those who entered.

Mr. Johnson led you through the labyrinth of shelves, his finger tracing along the spines of the books. He paused at one particular section, marked with a faded label that read "Euclid-class anomalies." He explained that these were objects of great complexity and were classified as having unpredictable behavior. you saw books with titles like "SCP-173: The Sculpture" and "SCP-682: The Hard-to-Destroy Reptile." you were filled with a mixture of trepidation and curiosity, eager to uncover the stories behind these peculiar entities.

Moving deeper into the Archive Room, you encountered rows of glass display cases. Inside each case lay carefully preserved artifacts, each with its own story to tell.You had beens drawn to a report, known as SCP-087: "First and Second report." Mr. Johnson explained that and you were allow to read the report!
